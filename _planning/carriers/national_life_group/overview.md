National Life Group (NLG) é uma carrier de seguros de vida e annuities americana fundada em 1848. Mutual holding (sem shareholders externos), trade name que cobre as subsidiárias National Life Insurance Company (NLIC, sede Vermont) e Life Insurance Company of the Southwest (LSW, sede Texas).

Posicionamento de mercado:

- **#2 maior provider de IUL nos EUA**.
- Assets consolidados $55,7B (12/31/2025); life insurance in force $421,5B; ~1,4M clientes.
- 2024: $635M life premium + $3,4B annuities (recorde).
- Forbes Best Insurance Companies 2024 e 2025; WSJ #2 Best Whole Life 2025.

Linha de produtos principal (Sparkbot deve sempre saber):

- **IUL (Indexed Universal Life)** — núcleo: FlexLife II (flagship middle market), PeakLife (premium funding), SummitLife (affluent), SurvivorLife (joint life estate), RapidProtect (instant decision $50K-$500K, lançado set/2025).
- **Whole Life** — TotalSecure (10-Pay, 15-Pay, 20-Pay, Paid-up at 65, Lifetime), BasicSecure (UL).
- **Term Life** — 10/15/20/30-year level term + ART, conversível para permanente até age 70.
- **Annuities** — FIT Secure Growth, FIT Select Income (FIA), Zenith series, RetireMax MYGAs.

Diferenciais únicos:

- **Living Benefits Riders sem custo adicional** em todos IULs — Terminal/Chronic/Critical Illness, Critical Injury, Alzheimer's (industry-first 2023), Fertility Journey (industry-first 2024).
- **Same-day digital issuance** via stack iPipeline (iGo eApp + Resonant UW + LexisNexis Risk Classifier).
- **Sales Desk dedicado** (live + advanced sales team).
