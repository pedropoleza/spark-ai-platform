                                                                                              INTERNAL EXCHANGE RULES

A replacement is rarely in the best interest of a consumer. It may subject a consumer to additional surrender
charges, new suicide and contestable periods, and tax liability. It is your responsibility as an agent to evaluate a
consumer’s needs holistically and, if they have existing coverage, determine if a replacement may or may not be
in their best interest.

National Life's Commission Policy applicable to inforce policies being transferred to new contracts or existing
contracts with an increase in coverage:

  Pay Full Commission to the Contract Target Premium (CTP)
  • After existing term contract has been in force for 7 years
  • After existing permanent contract has been in force for 8 years

  Pay Reduced Commission up to the CTP
  Prior to the existing contract’s 7th or 8th anniversary for term or permanent contracts, respectively. The amount
  of the reduced commission will depend on how long the contract has been in force and on the premium for the
  replacement contract compared to the premium of the contract being replaced.

  There will not be a premium load on the Internal 1035 money. No excess commissions will be paid.
