Resumo de outras condições médicas comuns pra UW NLG. Pra rating real, sempre XRAE field tool ou Sales Desk 800-906-3310.

**Hepatitis B / C:**

- "Hep B/C, chronic (treated and resolved)" → **qualifies RapidProtect** + possível Standard em full UW.
- Active untreated → rated heavy ou decline.
- APS auto-trigger pra hepatitis history.

**HIV / AIDS:**

- **NÃO qualifies pra RapidProtect**.
- Em full UW: HIV+ historicamente decline, mas com modern antiretroviral therapy (HAART) some carriers começam a oferecer rated. NLG specific policy [unverified — confirmar Sales Desk 800-906-3310].

**Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD):**

- **NÃO qualifies pra RapidProtect** ("Chronic Kidney Disease" non-qualifying).
- Em full UW: depende de stage (1-5), eGFR, dialysis status.
- Stage 1-2 stable: possível rated.
- Stage 4-5 ou dialysis: decline.

**Dialysis:**

- "Kidney failure requiring dialysis" → **RapidProtect non-qualifying**.
- Em full UW: typically decline.

**Polycystic Kidney Disease:**

- **RapidProtect non-qualifying**.

**Organ Transplant:**

- **NÃO qualifies RapidProtect** ("Organ Transplant, awaiting or recipient").
- Em full UW: depende de tipo (kidney best, liver mid, heart hardest), tempo desde transplant, current function.
- Kidney transplant >5 anos com clean function: possível heavy table.
- Recent (<2 anos) ou multiple organs: decline likely.

**Neurological:**

- **Parkinson's** → RapidProtect non-qualifying. Em full UW: rated heavy ou decline.
- **MS** → não listado explicitamente em RapidProtect; em full UW depende severity.
- **ALS** → RapidProtect non-qualifying. Em full UW: typically decline (terminal disease).
- **Alzheimer's / Dementia** → RapidProtect non-qualifying. Em full UW: decline likely (active diagnosis).
- **Cerebral Palsy** "(age >8 years, no epilepsy or intellectual disability)" → qualifies RapidProtect.
- **Epilepsy / seizures**:
  - Diagnosed within past 6 months → RapidProtect non-qualifying.
  - Em full UW: "Epilepsy (depends on age, treatment)" + "Seizures (mild, stable, average of ≤12 seizures per year, last seizure >1 year ago)" → possível rated.
  - Qualifies RapidProtect: "Epilepsy (depends on age, treatment)".
- **Down's syndrome / Intellectual Disability** → RapidProtect non-qualifying. Em full UW: case-by-case.
- **Paralysis** → RapidProtect non-qualifying.
- **Autism** → RapidProtect qualifying (mild, age >8, sem outras mental health/epilepsy).

**Crohn's disease:**

- "Crohn's, age >30, more than 5 years since diagnosis or last major attack" → possível rated.
- **NÃO qualifies RapidProtect** ("Crohn's Disease" na non-qualifying list).

**Ulcerative Colitis:**

- "Ulcerative Colitis (mild, diagnosis >6 months ago, no immunosuppressive medications)" → possível rated.
- **NÃO qualifies RapidProtect**.

**Lupus:**

- "Lupus, systemic (mild with no complications)" → qualifies RapidProtect.
- "Lupus, discoid (skin only; in remission, no steroid use)" → qualifies RapidProtect.
- Em full UW: rated em casos com órgão envolvimento.

**Rheumatoid Arthritis:**

- "Rheumatoid arthritis (no disability — depends on age)" → qualifies RapidProtect.
- "Rheumatoid arthritis (mild, depends on age & duration, no disability, only NSAID use)" → possível Standard em full UW.

**Other autoimmune:**

- "Autoimmune disease" listada como restricted med categoria EZ UW.
- Tratamento case-by-case.

**Cirrose / Liver disease:**

- "Cirrhosis of the liver" → **RapidProtect non-qualifying**.
- Em full UW: depende severity, etiologia (alcohol, hepatitis), liver function.
- Alcohol-related cirrhosis: rated heavy + abstinence required.

**Pacemaker / Defibrillator:**

- Listado em cardiac.md (chunk dedicado) — RapidProtect non-qualifying; full UW heavy.

**Comportamento Sparkbot:**

Pra qualquer condição:

1. Identifica se está em RapidProtect non-qualifying list (= use full UW path).
2. Pra full UW: APS auto-trigger comum.
3. Sugere XRAE pre-quote ou Sales Desk pra rating real.
4. Para condições não-listadas explicitamente: "não tenho info confiável; consulte Sales Desk 800-906-3310" (não inventa).
