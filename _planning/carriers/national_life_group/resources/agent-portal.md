Recursos disponíveis pra rep contracted com NLG. Sales Desk: **800-906-3310**.

**Agent Portal:** agent.nationallife.com

- **Lightning Bolt** — quick access pra tools mais usadas.
- **Case management** — status de cada app, comissões pendentes/payable.
- **Marketing Center** — campanhas pré-fabricadas (Sleep Well Client Campaign, Term-to-Perm Conversion eKit, etc).
- **Forms library** — todos cataloged forms.
- **Training modules** — NLGroupU integration.

**NLG ForeSight (illustration software):**

- Web e desktop. Help docs em https://www.nationallife.com/NWI/Help/en-US/Peach/Introduction.htm.
- Quick Quote disponível também via mobile app.
- Vai diretamente pro iGo eApp com illustration PDF anexado.

**iGo (eApp via iPipeline):**

- Application + e-signature + integração illustration.
- Yellow fields = required.

**Resonant (UW engine):**

- LexisNexis Risk Classifier integrated.
- Cross-checks MIB + Milliman + MVR + LabPiQture automaticamente.

**XRAE — Express Risk Assessment Exchange:**

- Field UW tool no portal pra pre-quote.
- "Additional Review Required" outcome → submeter via messaging tool, response em 1 business day.
- **NÃO disponível pra RapidProtect** (RapidProtect tem simplified UW próprio).

**NLG Agents Mobile App (iOS + Android):**

- iOS: https://apps.apple.com/us/app/nlg-agents/id964515750
- Android: https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.nlgroup.nlgagent
- Quick Quote (illustrations rápidas).
- Case status & tracking.
- **Commission calendar** + reports (pending + payable).
- Text notifications pra updates.

**Training:**

- **NLGroup University (NLGroupU)** — portal training video/online.
- **New Advisor Lift-Off Program** (virtual, novos reps).
- **Practice Management Conference** (year-2 producers).
- **The Edge Annual Summit** (top leaders).
- **Illustration & Case Design School**.
- On-site schools + virtual webinars.
- **Sales Desk live + advanced sales team** (poucas carriers têm dedicado).
- **CPA Advantage Program** — focus em parceria com contadores.
