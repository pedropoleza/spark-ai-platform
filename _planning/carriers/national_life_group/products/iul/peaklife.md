PeakLife (em NY: **PeakLife NL** via NLIC) é IUL Advanced Markets posicionado pra higher-net-worth focado em supplemental retirement / premium funding. Differente do FlexLife pelo target market e min face.

**Issue ages:** 18–85.
**Min face: $1.000.000** — produto pra premium funding, NÃO middle market.

**Faces & UW requirements (Issue Age Nearest Birthday):**

- $1M–$2M: ages 0–60 só Application, 61+ exige D (Application + Exam + Blood + Urine), 70+ exige ME/APS
- $2M–$3M: ages 41+ exigem D ou D/APS
- $5M+: ages 18–30 exigem D/APS, todas demais idades também

**Rate classes:** Elite, Preferred NT, Select NT, Standard NT, Express Standard NT 1 e 2 (Total Face ≤ $2M), Standard Tobacco, Preferred Tobacco, Express Standard Tobacco. Substandard até table H disponível.

**Financial UW thresholds (PeakLife):**

- Face $2M+ → E-Inspection
- Face $5M+ → Confidential Financial Questionnaire (Form 1392)
- Face $10M+ → Income Verification + Third Party Verified Financials

**Crediting strategies:** mesmas do FlexLife — S&P 500 P2P Cap Focus, Participation Focus, MSCI EAFE, Balanced Trend, US Pacesetter (sempre marcar como hypothetical/backtested em ilustração — lawsuit Virani), Systematic Allocation, Basic Strategy. Cap rates idem [unverified — confirmar Cap Update mais recente].

**Living Benefits Riders sem custo:** ABR set completo (Terminal/Chronic/Critical Illness/Critical Injury, Alzheimer, Fertility), LIBR (ages 60–85), Overloan Protection, Children's Term ($25K), Waiver of Premium.

**Posicionamento:**

- Cliente HNW com $1M+ target premium e accumulation focus
- Supplemental retirement income (cash value via loans/withdrawals)
- Pessoas em premium funding strategies, executive bonus, etc

**Quando NÃO usar:**

- Cliente middle market sem $1M target → use FlexLife (vai ficar underfunded em PeakLife)
- Cliente quer rapidez sem exam → não fit (PeakLife exige D/UW completo em quase todas idades)
- Cliente foca death benefit puro (não accumulation) → considere TotalSecure (WL) ou Term

**Fontes:** Underwriting Guide Cat 62797(0126) §22 (PeakLife/Advanced Markets IUL).
