NLG SureLC Agent Set up Procedure


  1. Five Rings Contracting Team will set up an account for the agent

  2. Agent will receive an email from setup@surancebay.com

  3. In the email, go to “Click Here”. The agent will be directed to SureLC website

  4. Agent will need to create a password- after that, agent will receive a
     conﬁrmation email from setup@surancebay.com titled “Activation
     Successful”

  5. Prompts to sign agreements and acknowledge SureLC policy- check box and
     click “accept policy”

  6. Agent will be directed to their proﬁle where they will ﬁll out their information

  7. On the left hand side bar, click on “Doing Business As” and select “Individual”
     under “Select your DBA type”

  8. Fill out the rest of the information following the prompts

  9. On the “Signature” tab, agents need to draw/upload their signatures


  10.    Look out for another email from contracts@surancebay.com titled
    “Contract Request - From Five Rings Financial LLC for National Life Group
    (NLG) (Independent)”

  11.    In the email, click “Start Your Review at SureLC” to review carrier
    questions and questionnaire to complete the contract

  12.     When the contract is ready to be signed off, the contracting team will get
    a notiﬁcation
